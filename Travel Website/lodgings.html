Lodgings in Disney World


Places to stay:

- The Polynesian Village Resort: a South Pacific-themed resort with world-class restaurants and palm trees.

- The Grand Floridian Resort and Spa: a Victorian-themed hotel with an orchestra in the lobby that plays jazz music and popular Disney toons!

- The Wilderness Lodge: a hotel inspired by the turn-of-the-century National Park lodges in America. It celebrates the beauty of nature and has an awesome pool with a slide.

- The Contemporary Resort: a hotel with spectacular views and dazzling pools and quick access to the Monorail.
There are many wonderful resorts and hotels to chose to stay at Disney World. We are committed to finding you the perfect place to stay during your stay!
